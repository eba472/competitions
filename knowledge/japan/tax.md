確定申告 kakutei (confirm)-shinkoku(report): Final income tax return

- in person, by post, e-tax online filing
- **Aoiro-Shinkoku 青色申告 (Blue Tax Return)**
- **Shiroiro shinkoku 白色申告 (White Tax Return)**
